Affiliate Sponsorship

INTUGLO’s affiliate sponsorship program’s objective is to bridge the gap and create a supportive ecosystem between our suppliers, partners and relevant players in the market. This shall serve as one of the key steps to build a cross border connectivity within an inclusive community. This program is expected to create a positive impact on the economy.

Our affiliate sponsorship program runs on a 1% contributing fund from our sales. It will be channelled directly to our affiliates and used for activities or programs that are aligned with our corporate objectives. It is also independent of the company’s marketing plan thus ensuring transparency and compliance. For more information on sponsorship requirements, please contact us at customerservice@intuglo.com .
