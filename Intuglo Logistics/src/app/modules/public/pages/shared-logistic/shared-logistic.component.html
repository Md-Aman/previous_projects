Shared Logistic

INTUGLO offers real time rates based on shared shipping. Our solution optimises the capacity offered by the container-sharing model. Your cargo is given a unique identification to ensure minimum contamination risk by segregating Halal from Non-Halal cargo.

Key benefits:
  • Aggregates logistics service providers based on real time information to increase visibility to both exporters and importers.
  • Optimises cargo loads, rates and delivery times.
  • Provides tracking and monitoring through a user-friendly real time dashboard.
  • HS Code as Identifier.
  • Shared Price Algorithm.
  • Pricing Dynamics.
  • Multi-channel Communication
  • Halal and Non-Halal Consolidation Search Capability.
  • Separate Incoterm for Ease of Packing.
  • Optimized Loading Capacity by Freight Types.
