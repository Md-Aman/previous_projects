Production Management

INTUGLO offers an easier and more reliable working environment for our partners with our production management tool. It has an in-build real time production tracker and compliance guidelines to ensure quality assurance from start to end. Our winning proposition is access to various production stages from ONE dashboard, anytime, anywhere.

Key benefits:
  • Provides visibility of validated OEMs for SMEs to ensure quality production.
  • Enhances communication efficiency between SMEs and OEMs.
  • Provides guidelines and frameworks to ensure compliance with product quality regulations and standards.
