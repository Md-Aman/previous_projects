Our Story

HBG Fluxr Digital Sdn Bhd was established in November 2016. Our core business is the development and design of innovative technology solutions for micro businesses and SMEs. We received our MSC status from MDEC in February 2017 with a patent pending for our logistic module system and algorithm.

Business owners, especially micro businesses and SMEs, continue to face challenges from financial management, market access, compliance and regulation, performance management to customer service. Supply chain planning is also difficult and requires companies to rethink their strategies and look at it as a technology requirement.

In early 2017, we began the development of INTUGLO, a digital single sign-on trade enabler platform that allows business owners to go global, faster and more cost-effective. It is a facilitation system that is built on our real time and on-time core technology.

Why INTUGLO

The INTUGLO platform is built on 8 modular integrated web applications using cloud infrastructure. What does this mean to business owners? With INTUGLO, they can now customised or personalised their market access and choose the business solutions that best meet their requirements to penetrate existing and new markets.

What can INTUGLO do for you?

INTUGLO is a single sign-on trade enabler platform that serves as a tool that meets all your trade needs. These include:

  • Logistics/shipping
  • Warehousing
  • Production management
  • Quality Control (QCs) for hire
  • Online exhibition or 24/7 showcase
  • E-procurement
  • Market validation
  • Project management

INTUGLO advantage

The seamless integration of 8 modular web applications is designed on a shared economy or collaborative consumption model. It works similar to a supply chain management with the added advantage of an elevated brand reputation management.

INTUGLO is FREE to sign-up and offered on a ‘PAY-PER-USE’ pricing model. Our users only pay for what they use and when they use it. This allows small businesses to scale globally without incurring high IT cost and minimize the risk of overconsumption.

INTUGLO is the only platform to meet the needs of both the HALAL and NON-HALAL industries. Our system has the built-in capability to fully segregate the halal products throughout the entire supply chain. We contribute to and support Malaysia’s vision to be the largest Halal Trade Gateway for ASEAN by 2020.
